Guide

Basics

Objectiv of the Game

The primary goal is to reduce your opponent's Health from 100 to 0. You achieve this by summoning creatures, casting powerful spells, and strategically managing your resources to outmaneuver your opponent.

Turn Structure

    Each player's turn is divided into three main phases:

  1. Draw Phase:At the start of your turn, you draw one card from your deck.
  2. Main Phase:This is where you'll take most of your actions. You can play creature cards, cast spells, and equip artifacts.
  3. Combat Phase:You may declare attacks with your creatures that are able to attack. Your opponent then has a chance to declare blockers.

Tip: Resource Management

Your core resource is Mana, which replenishes and increases by one each turn. Planning your Mana usage across several turns is key to victory. Don't spend it all at once if you have a powerful card you want to play next turn!

Card Rarity

Common

These cards are the most frequently found in booster packs and are the backbone of many decks. They are versatile and easy to obtain, making them ideal for beginners.

Rare

Rare cards are hard to find and typically have powerful effects or unique abilities. They can be game-changers and are highly sought after by collectors and competitive players alike.

Epic

Epic cards are very rare and often feature extraordinary abilities that can turn the tide of a game. They are prized possessions for any player and can be the centerpiece of a deck.

Legendary

Legendary cards are the rarest and most powerful cards in the game. They often have unique lore and abilities that set them apart from all other cards. Owning a legendary card is a mark of prestige among players.

Mythic

Mythic cards are beyond legendary in rarity and power. They often feature game-altering abilities and are typically limited edition or promotional cards. Mythic cards are the ultimate prize for collectors and competitive players.

Card Guide

FAQ

How many cards can I play per turn?

You can play up to 3 cards per turn, provided each card is of a different type. In certain strategic situations, if you play your cards correctly, you may be able to play up to 5 cards in a single turn.

How do I win the game?

Victory is achieved by reducing your opponent's health points to zero. You can do this through strategic card combinations, powerful attacks, and defensive maneuvers that protect your own health while damaging your opponent.

What are the different card types?

There are several card types including Attack cards, Defense cards, Spell cards, and Special Effect cards. Each type has unique abilities and can be combined strategically to create powerful combos during gameplay.

Can I play with friends online?

Yes! The game features full online multiplayer support. You can invite friends to private matches, join ranked competitive games, or practice against AI opponents to improve your skills before challenging other players.
