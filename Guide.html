Guide

Basics

Objectiv of the Game

The primary goal is to reduce your opponent's Health from 100 to 0. You achieve this by summoning creatures, casting powerful spells, and strategically managing your resources to outmaneuver your opponent.

Turn Structure

    Each player's turn is divided into three main phases:

  1. Draw Phase:At the start of your turn, you draw one card from your deck.
  2. Main Phase:This is where you'll take most of your actions. You can play creature cards, cast spells, and equip artifacts.
  3. Combat Phase:You may declare attacks with your creatures that are able to attack. Your opponent then has a chance to declare blockers.

Tip: Resource Management

Your core resource is Mana, which replenishes and increases by one each turn. Planning your Mana usage across several turns is key to victory. Don't spend it all at once if you have a powerful card you want to play next turn!
